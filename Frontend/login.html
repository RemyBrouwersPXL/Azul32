How to Play Azul

×

Game Overview

Azul is a tile-placement game where players compete to create the most beautiful mosaic on their wall.

Getting Started

  1. Create an account or sign in
  2. Join a game room or create your own
  3. Invite friends or wait for other players
  4. When the game starts, you'll see the factory displays filled with tiles

Game Rules

  • On your turn, take all tiles of one color from a factory display
  • Place these tiles on one of the pattern lines on your board
  • When a row is complete, one tile moves to your wall
  • Score points based on tile placement
  • The game ends when a player completes a horizontal row on their wall
